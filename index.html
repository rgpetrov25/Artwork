Statement

The art displayed on this page is reflective of my interests and inspirations as an artist. I often draw from personal experiences for inspiration, as well as the environment around me. Much of the visuals in my art is inspired by nature, as many artists already are. I often consider the intention of my work so far, but it is safe to say that at this moment I am still in development. My theme for this period of life is change and being consistently inconsistent. I desire to try everything and to explore ideas that I could combine in the future. My aim is to accumulate a portfolio showcasing a wide range of my abilities and visual style, that references the human spirit, consciousness, and change as a concept. I often use pencils and markers, as well as paint and pastels. Much of my work dabbles in color, as I am also inspired by graffiti art. I usually take a holistic approach to making art, considering every stroke and line as its own unit of constructing a greater image. I am also inspired by phenomenalism, the idea that our conscious reality is mainly interpreted through our sensations.
